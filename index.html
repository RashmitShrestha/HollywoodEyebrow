We specialise in eyebrow threading. A centuries old Indian technique, threading is a swift and very accurate way to shape brows. To ensure perfectly sculpted eyebrows, a cotton thread is used to glide across the skin, removing excess hair while it does so. Not only that but the thread grabs and teases out every single hair by its root leaving a cleaner finish with longer lasting results. The results are wonderfully neat and perfectly symmetrical eyebrows which can take years off your face.
